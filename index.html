The Project's Client...

We Stay/ Nos Quedamos (NQ) is a New York based nonprofit community development corporation. We Stay/Nos Quedamos has been developing affordable housing in the Melrose section of the South Bronx for over three decades, and is interested in establishing a community land trust (CLT), run by a non-profit entity, that will ensure that local residents remain the decision-making stewards for the neighborhood.

Objects of The Project...

NQ is pursuing the establishment of a community land trust to address the lack of affordable housing in the South Bronx, and create a more intentional, equitable community fabric in the area. NQ engaged a Capstone team to aid in the establishment of the land trust through a three-prong implementation assessment.

Part of The Analysis...

NQ planned to incorporate commercial stores in the CLT. NYU Capstone team, therefore, analyzed the existing legally operating business in community district 1 & 2 & 3.
